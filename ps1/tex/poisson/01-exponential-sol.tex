\[ p(y;\eta) = b(y)exp[\eta^T T(y) - a(\eta)] \]
We can fit the terms in the function as:
\[ b(y) = \frac{1}{y^!} \]
\[ \eta = log\lambda \]
\[ T(y) = y \]
\[ a(\eta) = e^\eta \]
